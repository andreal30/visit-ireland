Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Dublin Arrival: Arrive in Dublin, the vibrant capital of Ireland. Settle into your comfortable accommodation and prepare to embark on a journey of discovery.
  2. Day 2 - Introduction to Irish Genealogy: Meet your dedicated genealogy guide and embark on an introductory session. Learn about Irish genealogy research methods, explore online resources, and gain valuable insights into tracing your family history. Discuss your ancestral background and receive personalized research guidance.
  3. Day 3 - Research Day in National Archives: Delve into the treasure trove of information housed within the National Archives of Ireland. With the support of your guide, navigate historical records like birth certificates, census data, and church registers. Uncover fascinating details about your ancestors' lives, piecing together the puzzle of your family's past.
  4. Day 4 - Ancestral Town Visit & Local Records: Travel to the town or village where your ancestors once resided. Immerse yourself in the local atmosphere and explore historical landmarks that may have shaped their lives. Visit local churches and archives to access parish records and other documents that shed light on your family's history. Connect with local historians and genealogical societies who may offer additional insights.
  5. Day 5 - Heritage Center Exploration & Cultural Show: Gain a deeper understanding of Irish history and culture at a dedicated heritage center. Through interactive exhibits and displays, immerse yourself in the traditions and way of life of your ancestors. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Irish cultural show, featuring lively music, dance, and storytelling. Experience the warmth and vibrancy of Irish heritage firsthand.
  6. Day 6 - Day Trip to Historical Sites: Embark on a day trip to explore historical sites relevant to your family's story. This could include ancient castles, battlefields, or famine graveyards, depending on your research findings. Gain a deeper understanding of the historical context that shaped your ancestors' lives.
  7. Day 7 - Dublin Departure with Personalized Report: Reflect on your transformative journey as you prepare for your departure from Dublin. Receive a comprehensive personalized report summarizing your family history research findings, including documented discoveries, potential leads for further research, and historical context. Take home a newfound appreciation for your Irish roots and a deeper connection to your ancestral homeland.
