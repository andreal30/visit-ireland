Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Dublin Arrival & National Leprechaun Museum: Arrive in Dublin, Ireland's vibrant capital. Settle into your comfortable family-friendly accommodation and prepare for a sprinkle of magic. Visit the National Leprechaun Museum, a whimsical and interactive experience that brings Irish folklore to life. Learn about mischievous leprechauns, explore hidden fairy dwellings, and embark on a quest to find a pot of gold (or at least a photo opportunity with one!).
  2. Day 2 - Dublin Zoo & Trinity College: No family adventure is complete without a visit to the zoo! Dublin Zoo is a haven for wildlife, with over 600 animals from around the globe. Witness majestic lions, playful penguins, and a whole lot more as you explore the various enclosures. In the afternoon, delve into Irish history and academia with a visit to Trinity College, home to the Book of Kells, a beautifully illuminated manuscript dating back to the 9th century. Explore the historic campus and uncover the rich academic heritage of this prestigious university.
  3. Day 3 - Galway City & Connemara Day Trip: Journey west to Galway, a charming city renowned for its lively atmosphere, traditional pubs, and rich cultural heritage. Explore the bustling shops at Galway Market, enjoy a delicious seafood lunch overlooking the harbor, and lose yourself in the city's vibrant energy. In the afternoon, continue your adventure to Connemara, a region known for its dramatic landscapes, quaint villages, and traditional way of life. Take a scenic drive along the Wild Atlantic Way, stopping at scenic viewpoints and charming villages along the way. This day trip offers a glimpse into the natural beauty and rich culture of western Ireland.
  4. Day 4 - Ashford Castle & Falconry Show: Step into a fairytale with a visit to Ashford Castle, a luxurious castle hotel nestled amidst stunning scenery. Explore the castle's magnificent grounds, admire its opulent interiors (entrance fee may apply), or simply relax in the beautiful gardens. In the afternoon, immerse yourselves in the fascinating world of falconry with a thrilling falconry show. Witness skilled falconers demonstrate the hunting techniques of these magnificent birds and learn about their unique characteristics. This interactive experience is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser for all ages.
  5. Day 5 - Croagh Patrick Hike (optional) & Westport House: For the adventurous families, conquer the challenging climb of Croagh Patrick, Ireland's holy mountain. This optional activity offers breathtaking panoramic views and a sense of accomplishment. For those seeking a more relaxed pace, explore Westport House, a grand 18th-century mansion with beautiful gardens and a fascinating history. Dress up in period costumes, take a tour of the grand rooms, and learn about the life of the wealthy Westport family.
  6. Day 6 - Dublin Departure & Farewell Dinner: Reflect on your incredible family adventure as you enjoy a scenic transfer back to Dublin Airport. Bid farewell to Ireland with a final celebratory dinner at a family-friendly restaurant in Dublin. Savor delicious Irish cuisine and share stories and laughter about the unforgettable memories you created together.
