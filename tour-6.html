Puffin Palooza: West Coast Wildlife Adventure

Witness the Magic of Marine Life in Ireland

General Description

Calling all nature lovers! This 5-day adventure along Ireland's rugged west coast is designed to bring you face-to-face with incredible wildlife and breathtaking scenery. From the playful antics of puffins to the majesty of whales, prepare to be amazed by the natural wonders of the Emerald Isle.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1- Dublin to Kenmare: Kick off your adventure with a scenic drive from Dublin to Kenmare, a charming town nestled amidst the stunning landscapes of County Kerry. Settle into your comfortable accommodation and get ready to explore the wonders of the west coast.
  2. Day 2- Skellig Islands Boat Trip: Embark on an unforgettable boat trip to the Skellig Islands, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. These remote islands are home to a vast puffin colony, with thousands of these charismatic birds nesting on the cliffs during the breeding season (April-August). Keep your eyes peeled for other marine life such as dolphins, whales, and porpoises as you navigate the Atlantic waters. Explore the islands' monastic ruins, steeped in history and offering stunning views.
  3. Day 3 - Kerry Way Hiking & Dingle Peninsula: Lace up your boots and embark on a hike along a section of the Kerry Way, a renowned walking trail offering panoramic views of rolling hills, rugged coastlines, and charming villages. In the afternoon, continue your journey to the Dingle Peninsula, a haven for marine life. This beautiful area is known for its dramatic cliffs, pristine beaches, and abundance of wildlife, including whales, dolphins, and seals. Keep your camera ready as you explore the peninsula's hidden coves and charming villages.
  4. Day 4: Fungie the Dolphin & Dingle Town: Encounter a local legend! Take a boat trip in Dingle Bay in search of Fungie, a bottlenose dolphin who has become a beloved resident of the area for decades. In the afternoon, explore the vibrant town of Dingle, known for its colorful pubs, traditional music scene, and friendly atmosphere. Browse local craft shops, savor fresh seafood in a waterfront restaurant, and soak up the charm of this Irish gem.
  5. Day 5: Kenmare to Dublin: Enjoy a scenic drive back to Dublin, reflecting on the incredible wildlife encounters and breathtaking landscapes you experienced throughout your adventure.
