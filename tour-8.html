Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Dublin Arrival & Bike Fitting: Arrive in Dublin, the vibrant capital of Ireland. Settle into your comfortable accommodation and get fitted for your trusty steed - a high-quality bike perfectly suited for your cycling adventure.
  2. Day 2 - Dublin to Galway (transfer) & Connemara Cycle (moderate): Kick off your adventure with a scenic transfer from Dublin to Galway, a charming city on Ireland's west coast. After storing your luggage, embark on your first cycle through the Connemara region. Moderate terrain with rolling hills and scenic byways will lead you past quaint villages, traditional thatched cottages, and along the Atlantic coastline. Keep your eyes peeled for dramatic cliffs, hidden coves, and charming pubs where you can refuel with a refreshing drink and traditional Irish fare.
  3. Day 3 - Connemara National Park Cycle (easy): Delve deeper into the heart of Connemara National Park on a leisurely cycling route. Easy terrain with minimal elevation gain allows you to soak in the beauty of the park's diverse landscapes. Cycle through lush forests, past shimmering lakes, and witness the majestic spectacle of Connemara's rugged mountains. Enjoy stops at scenic viewpoints and historical sites, capturing unforgettable memories of your journey.
  4. Day 4 - Ferry to Kerry & Cycle to Killarney (moderate): Catch a ferry across the Shannon Estuary, embarking on the next chapter of your cycling adventure. Upon arrival in County Kerry, a moderate cycling route awaits, leading you towards the charming town of Killarney, nestled amidst the stunning scenery of Killarney National Park. Enjoy scenic backroads, rolling hills, and the ever-present beauty of the Irish countryside.
  5. Day 5 - Ring of Kerry Cycle (challenging): Gear up for the most challenging leg of your journey - the iconic Ring of Kerry cycle. This route winds its way along dramatic coastlines, offering breathtaking ocean views, towering cliffs, and charming villages. While the terrain is demanding with steeper climbs and descents, the reward is unparalleled. Stop at scenic viewpoints to capture panoramic vistas and savor the sense of accomplishment as you conquer this iconic route.
  6. Day 6 - Killarney National Park Cycle (easy): Take a breather with a relaxing cycle through Killarney National Park. Easy terrain allows you to fully appreciate the park's diverse beauty. Explore Killarney's majestic lakes, spot wildlife like deer and red foxes, and discover hidden waterfalls and historical ruins. This leisurely ride is a perfect way to unwind and reflect on the incredible experiences of your cycling adventure.
  7. Day 7 - Killarney to Dublin (transfer) & Departure: Enjoy a scenic transfer back to Dublin, reflecting on the unforgettable memories you've created throughout your cycling adventure. Bid farewell to Ireland with a newfound appreciation for its stunning scenery, charming villages, and warm hospitality.
