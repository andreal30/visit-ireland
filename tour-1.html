Dublin Delights

Immerse yourself in the heart of Ireland!

General Description

Immerse yourself in the vibrant capital. Explore Dublin Castle, Trinity College (home to the Book of Kells), and the Guinness Storehouse. Enjoy a traditional pub dinner and an evening of Irish music.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - City Tour & Trinity College: Embark on a guided city tour, marveling at architectural wonders like Dublin Castle and St. Patrick's Cathedral. Immerse yourself in the intellectual heart of Ireland with a visit to Trinity College, home to the Book of Kells, a stunning illuminated manuscript.
  2. Day 2 - Guinness Storehouse & Kilmainham Gaol: Unleash your inner hophead at the Guinness Storehouse, learning the secrets of Ireland's iconic stout through interactive exhibits and tastings. Travel back in time at Kilmainham Gaol, a former prison with a poignant history, and hear tales of Irish rebels and revolutionaries.
  3. Day 3 - St. Stephen's Green & Pub Night: Stroll through the lush oasis of St. Stephen's Green, a haven of peace in the bustling city center. Cap off your adventure with a traditional pub dinner, savoring hearty Irish fare and enjoying a lively evening of Irish music. You might even catch a session of trad music, with fiddles, flutes, and bodhráns filling the air.
