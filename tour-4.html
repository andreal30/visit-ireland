Northern Ireland Highlights

A Journey Through History, Nature, and Pop Culture

General Description

Unveil the captivating tapestry of Northern Ireland on this immersive 6-day adventure. Explore Belfast's vibrant energy, marvel at the geological wonder of the Giant's Causeway, and delve into the rich history etched on Derry's city walls. Walk in the footsteps of legendary characters from Game of Thrones filming locations, and experience the unique blend of history and natural beauty that defines Northern Ireland.

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Dublin to Belfast: Embark on your journey with a scenic drive from Dublin to Belfast, the dynamic capital of Northern Ireland. Settle into your comfortable accommodation and get ready to explore this vibrant city.
  2. Day 2 - Titanic Belfast & City Tour: Immerse yourself in Belfast's maritime heritage with a visit to Titanic Belfast, a state-of-the-art museum dedicated to the ill-fated RMS Titanic. Explore interactive exhibits, learn about the ship's construction and tragic fate, and gain insights into Belfast's shipbuilding industry. In the afternoon, embark on a guided city tour, discovering iconic landmarks like Belfast City Hall and exploring the historic neighborhoods.
  3. Day 3 - Giant's Causeway & Antrim Coast: Travel north to the awe-inspiring Giant's Causeway, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Marvel at these hexagonal basalt columns, formed by volcanic eruptions millions of years ago. Explore the dramatic coastline, witness stunning sea views, and uncover the legends surrounding this natural wonder. Continue your journey along the Antrim Coast, a designated Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty, capturing breathtaking scenery and charming villages along the way.
  4. Day 4 - Derry City Walls & Peace Murals: Journey to Derry, a historic city renowned for its resilience. Walk along the Derry City Walls, the most complete set of fortified walls in Ireland, offering panoramic views and a glimpse into the city's turbulent past. Explore the vibrant Bogside neighborhood, adorned with colorful murals depicting the city's civil rights struggles and the peace process.
  5. Day 5 - Game of Thrones Filming Locations: Calling all Game of Thrones fans! Embark on a captivating journey to filming locations used in the popular series. Stand in awe at the Dark Hedges, a spectacular avenue of beech trees, and explore other iconic locations that brought Westeros to life. Relive your favorite scenes and create unforgettable memories.
  6. Day 6 - Belfast to Dublin: Bid farewell to Northern Ireland with a scenic drive back to Dublin. Reflect on the unique experiences you've gathered throughout your adventure.
