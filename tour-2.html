Itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Dublin to Galway: Kick off your adventure with a scenic journey from Dublin to Galway, Ireland's bohemian capital. Soak up the city's vibrant atmosphere and explore its charming streets at your own pace.
  2. Day 2 - Connemara National Park & Kylemore Abbey: Travel westward to Connemara National Park, a haven of soaring mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and vast expanses of bogland. Hike along scenic trails, breathe in the fresh air, and marvel at the park's raw beauty. In the afternoon, explore the captivating Kylemore Abbey, a Benedictine monastery nestled amidst breathtaking scenery. Uncover its intriguing history and admire the architecture.
  3. Day 3 - Aran Islands Ferry & Exploration: Take a ferry to the Aran Islands, a captivating archipelago known for its ancient Gaelic culture, traditional way of life, and stunning natural beauty. Explore the islands by bike or horse-drawn carriage, visit historical sites like prehistoric forts, and experience the warm hospitality of the islanders. In the evening, return to Galway with unforgettable memories.
  4. Day 4 - Cliffs of Moher & The Burren: Prepare to be awestruck by the majestic Cliffs of Moher, towering 700 feet over the churning Atlantic Ocean. Witness panoramic coastal views and feel the spray of the sea on your face. In the afternoon, journey to the Burren, a unique karst landscape with a surprising abundance of wildflowers growing amidst the rocky terrain. Explore ancient dolmens and tombs, remnants of a bygone era.
  5. Day 5 - Galway City Exploration: Bid farewell to the Wild Atlantic Way with a final exploration of Galway City. Wander through the lively streets, browse the bustling shops, and soak up the city's artistic energy. Enjoy a farewell dinner, reminiscing about your adventures on the west coast of Ireland.
